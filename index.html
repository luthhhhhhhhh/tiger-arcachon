The Venue

GOLF
INTERNATIONAL
D'ARCACHON

La Teste-de-Buch · Gironde · France

Established in 1895 and redesigned to 18 holes by English architect Cecil Blandford in 1960, the course winds through a century-old maritime pine forest with panoramic views over the Bassin d'Arcachon from its historic Belle Époque clubhouse. The outward nine (T1–9) is hilly and wooded; the inward nine (T10–18) is flatter with strategic water hazards throughout.
